# FAQ

## How Frequently is Kosh Updated?

Kosh is a collection of multiple datasets. These datasets are
updated at different frequencies. Datasets opened by researchers
along with an academic paper are one time uploads. Other datasets,
such as the fact-checking sites data are updated weekly. If you
would like a dataset to be included in Kosh, please email us at
admin@tattle.co.in or reach out to us on Twitter (@tattlemade)
